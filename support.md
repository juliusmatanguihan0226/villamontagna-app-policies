COMMON QUESTIONS

Q: How do I book the villa?
A: Tap an available date in the Calendar tab, then choose "Request to Book on
Airbnb" or "Message us on Facebook."

Q: The calendar shows a date as available, but I see it booked elsewhere.
A: Our calendar pulls live from Airbnb every time you open the tab. If
something looks off, tap the refresh button in the top-right corner. New
bookings may take a few minutes to appear.

Q: Can I check in early or check out late?
A: Standard check-in is 2:00 PM and check-out is 11:00 AM. Early/late requests
must be approved in advance and may incur a fee. Message us before your stay
to ask.

Q: What's included in the rental?
A: See the Amenities and Kitchen & Dining sections of the About tab for a
detailed list, including the private pool, BBQ grill, WiFi, smart TV, kitchen
inventory, and complimentary welcome drinks.

Q: How do I get there?
A: Tap the Address row on the Contact tab and choose Waze, Google Maps, or
Apple Maps. The app will open the map directly at our coordinates in Lipa
City, Batangas.
