Privacy Policy — Villa Montagna

Effective date: May 28, 2026

Villa Montagna ("we", "our", or "the app") is a free informational app for our
vacation rental property in Lipa City, Batangas, Philippines. We respect your
privacy. This policy explains what we do — and don't — do with information when
you use the app.

1. Information We Collect

The Villa Montagna app does not collect, store, or transmit any personal
information about you. We do not require account creation, we do not use
analytics or advertising SDKs, and we do not track your activity inside or
outside the app.

2. Information You Provide

The app does not ask you to enter personal details. If you choose to contact
us through links inside the app (Facebook, Instagram, Messenger, email, or
Airbnb), any information you share is handled by those third-party services
under their own privacy policies, not ours.

3. Calendar Data

The app fetches public availability data from our Airbnb iCal calendar to
display booked and available dates. This data describes the property's
availability only — it does not contain any guest names, contact details, or
reservation specifics. The fetch happens on your device; we never see that you
opened the calendar.

4. Map and Web Links

When you tap an address, social link, or "View on Airbnb" button, the app
hands you off to Waze, Google Maps, Apple Maps, Facebook, Instagram, or
Airbnb. Once you leave the app, those services govern your data.

5. Children's Privacy

The app is rated 4+ and does not knowingly collect information from anyone,
including children under 13.

6. Changes

If we ever change how the app handles data, we will update this page and the
"Effective date" above before the change takes effect.
